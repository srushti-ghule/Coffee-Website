Refund Policy

Effective Date: 09/06/2025

1. Overview

At Brew & Bites, we strive to provide the best experience and service to our customers. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds will be provided for purchases made in-store or online (if applicable).

2. Food and Beverage Orders (In-Store)

All food and drink orders placed in-store are prepared fresh and are generally non-refundable once served. However, refunds or replacements may be considered in the following cases:

  • Wrong item delivered
  • Item not as described
  • Quality concerns reported immediately after serving

To be eligible, issues must be brought to the attention of staff before leaving the café.

3. Online Orders / Deliveries

If your order was placed online through our website or a delivery platform, you may be eligible for a refund if:

  • The item was not delivered
  • You received an incorrect or damaged item

Refund requests must be made within 24 hours of delivery. Please include your order number, photos (if applicable), and a clear explanation of the issue.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Partially consumed food or beverages
  • Change of mind after placing the order
  • Late pickup or delivery due to customer unavailability

5. Refund Process

If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 3–7 business days to your original payment method.
